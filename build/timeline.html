Timeline

  • 28 April 2021

    The project team starts sharing trail operation videos at Youtube channel

  • 19 January 2021

    Russian Federal Agency for Maritime and River Transport has issued Rules for application of the COLREGs-72 by autonomous vessels

  • 12 December 2020

    The Russian Maritime Register of Shipping approved the a-Nav set of technical solutions in principle

  • 08 December 2020

    The first two shipping companies sign the MOI for a-Nav installation on 20 vessels starting from 2021

  • 05 December 2020

    The Government Decree on wide national experiment on a-Navigation is approved

  • 05 October 2020

    Trials in real conditions are started

  • 15 September 2020

    Installation is completed in accordance with design approved by Russian Maritime Register of Shipping

  • 07 August 2020

    Shore based tests are completed

  • 20 March 2020

    Experimental equipment is manufactured

  • 10 March 2020

    IMO is informed about a-Navigation trail project (ARNTP) and upcoming national legislation changes

  • 20 February 2020

    Draft Law and Decree are prepared by Minpromtorg

  • 25 December 2019

    Proposals for Law and Decree are developed

  • 25 October 2019

    COLREGs-72 application to automatic navigation is developed by expert group and reviewed by FAMRT

  • 30 August 2019

    Risk assessment is completed

  • 25 July 2019

    Prototypes are developed and presented

  • 19 March 2019

    Basic methodology, System architecture and specifications are developed

  • 30 January 2019

    a-Nav project initiated by a group of companies, established a joint working group, the concept development is started

  • 20 November 2018

    Alexander Pinskiy has announced about a-Nav project at Transport Week Forum
